Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Pi Network Ecosystem App
Kirana Manager stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information.

1. About This App

Kirana Manager ("the App") is a grocery shop management tool built for small shop owners. It operates within the Pi Network ecosystem and is designed to run fully offline with no backend server.

2. Data We Collect

We collect no data. All information you enter — products, sales, expenses, shop name, settings — is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage on your own device.

  • No account creation required
  • No login or sign-in
  • No data sent to any server
  • No analytics or tracking
  • No cookies for tracking purposes
  • No third-party data sharing

3. Currency Exchange Rates

If you tap "Refresh live rates" in Settings, the App makes a single request to open.er-api.com — a free, no-key public API — to fetch current exchange rates. No personal information is sent in this request. This feature is entirely optional.

4. Pi Network Integration

This App is built for the Pi Network ecosystem. It does not currently process Pi payments or access the Pi SDK. Any future Pi payment integration will be disclosed in an updated version of this policy, and Pi Network's own Privacy Policy will apply to Pi-related interactions.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data lives in localStorage in your browser. It is accessible only to you on your device. Clearing your browser data or uninstalling the app will permanently delete all stored data. We recommend using the Export feature (Settings → Export Data) to back up your data regularly.

6. Children's Privacy

This App is intended for shop owners and business users. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact

For any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the Pi Network app ecosystem or at the developer's Pi Network profile.
